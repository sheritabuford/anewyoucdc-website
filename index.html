Who we are

A nonprofit built around dignity, opportunity, and lasting change.

A New You Community Development Coalition is a community-centered nonprofit helping individuals and families move from uncertainty to stability. We combine safe housing, educational guidance, personal development, business consulting, and trusted resource connections into a clear path forward.

Our work is rooted in the belief that every person deserves to be seen, supported, and equipped to build a healthier future. We welcome clients, families, referral partners, property owners, agencies, and community stakeholders who share that vision.

Frequently asked questions

Answers before you take the next step.

Who does A New You CDC serve?

We support adults, families, entrepreneurs, property owners, and community partners seeking housing, education, consulting, personal development, or connections to appropriate resources.

How do I apply for housing?

Complete the contact form and select “Housing inquiry.” Our team will follow up regarding availability, pricing, screening, required documents, and the appropriate next step.

What housing accommodations are included?

Available features may include furnished rooms, Wi-Fi, security monitoring, keyless entry, air conditioning, laundry access, fresh linens, parking, and transportation considered case-by-case.

Do you accept agency and community referrals?

Yes. Referral partners may use the contact form and select “Community or agency referral.” Placement remains subject to availability, screening, program fit, and applicable requirements.

Can I schedule a consultation?

Yes. Select “Consulting” on the form for business development, credit and wealth education, real estate planning, or personal-growth support.

How can an organization partner with A New You CDC?

We welcome conversations with agencies, nonprofits, employers, funders, property owners, and service providers. Select “Partnership” on the contact form and describe your proposed collaboration.
