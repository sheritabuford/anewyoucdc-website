Important messages

Please review before your next appointment.

Protect your personal information

Do not email Social Security numbers, full financial account numbers, identification records, medical documents, passwords, or PINs. Submit sensitive documents only through an approved secure portal provided by your representative.

Appointments require confirmation

Calendar selections and email requests are not confirmed until A New You CDC responds. For confirmed availability, use the Square booking link below.

Complete your current phase

Review your previous action steps, prepare requested documents, and bring your questions so your scheduled time can remain focused and productive.

Keep your information current

Notify the team when your telephone number, email address, mailing address, business information, housing situation, or primary goals change.
